What is Referencing and why its important?

Referencing is a standardised method of acknowledging sources of information and ideas that you have used in your assignments, in a way that uniquely identifies their source. Direct quotations, facts and figures, as well as ideas and theories, from both published and unpublished works, must be referenced.

Explore our website to learn how to reference websites using the Harvard Referencing style and generate accurate reference lists for your assignments!

How to reference websites

The basic format to reference a website is: Author, Initials. (Year). Title of webpage. [Online] Available at: URL [Accessed: date].

For example:

Reference list:

Smith, J. (2019). How to reference websites. [Online] Available at: https://www.library.unisa.edu.au/referencing-roadmap/referencing-websites/ [Accessed: 10/10/2022].
